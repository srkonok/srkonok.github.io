About Me

Hello, I'm Shahriar Konok and Software Engineer and Cloud Practitioner

I'm distinguished graduate from Adamjee Cantonment College and Bangladesh Army University of Science and Technology, where I achieved GPA of 5 in both SSC and HSC, and a commendable CGPA of 3.6 out of 4 upon graduation. My fervor for technology led me to victory in the bdapps National Hackathon 2022 with the creation of the 'Mess Monitor' app, simplifying meal tracking for students and bachelors. At Shadin Lab LLC, I thrived as a Cloud Solution Developer, adeptly utilizing AWS services like EC2, S3, RDS, VPC, and Lambda to develop scalable applications. My commitment to continuous learning is evident through my specialized training in Cloud Management at the BASIS Institute of Technology and Management. My noteworthy involvement in government projects, such as managing beneficiary training for Rural Cooperative Societies (RDCD) using Laravel Microservices, underscores my versatility and dedication. Now, I eagerly seek new challenges in the tech industry, poised to leverage my expertise for impactful contributions.
